Overview of AMR

Confronting a Global Threat

so, when microorganism like bacteria and fungi develops the ability to resist treatments and drugs to which initially which were used to killed them, this is simply known as antimicrobial resistance or AMR. this change in microorganism increases the diseases duration by making them more difficult to get rid of them or basically treat them.

AMR, also known as hidden pandemic or silent pandemic , as it poses a threat to the foundation for modern medicine. the hazards of chemotherapy, various routine operations, as well as organ transplantation increases significantly in the absence of enough antimicrobials.

the goal of this platform is to encourage the cooperation and raise awareness for AMR. in order to overcome the serious problem of AMR, we want to gather together scientists , ordinary people as well as medical professionals.
